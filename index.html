What is Social Media?

Social media refers to internet-based platforms that allow users to create, share, and interact with content (text, images, videos) in the form of online social networks.

  • Two-way interaction between users
  • Content creation by users
  • Connection between individuals or groups
  • Easy access through mobile devices or computers

Evolution of Social Media

Timeline:

  • Early 2000s: Rise of MySpace and Facebook
  • 2010s: Mobile apps like Instagram and Snapchat
  • 2020s: Dominance of TikTok and video content

Benefits of Social Media

  • Communication and Connection: Facilitates global interaction
  • Learning Opportunities: Access to courses, webinars, and educational materials
  • Marketing and Business: Promotes products and services
